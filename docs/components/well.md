title: Well

Wells can be used for sectioning of a section of content.

### Standard well

<div class="well">
  Insert content here
</div>

<br />

### Well with some cells

<div class="well well--no-padding">
  <div class="well__cell">
    <span>Cell One</span>
  </div>
  <div class="well__cell">
    <span>Cell Two</span>
  </div>
  <div class="well__cell">
    <span>Cell Three</span>
  </div>
</div>

```html
<div class="well">

</div>
```
